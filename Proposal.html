Project Proposal

Problem Description

A previous attempt at utilizing an online health platform that is designed to help users evaluate and understand their health symptoms is WebMD. This website allows users to input the symptoms an individual is experiencing, ask questions to narrow down the possibilities, produce possible conditions, and read information on possible causes and symptoms. It provides general content and suggestions based on the user's symptoms without taking into account an individual's medical history. However, there is potential for improvements like more customizability features for the users, incorporating the context of a user's history, and providing more relevant information. Overall, WebMD has attempted to provide individuals with an online space to seek guidance in understanding health indicators.
